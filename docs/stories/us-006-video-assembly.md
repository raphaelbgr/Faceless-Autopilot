# US-006: Video Assembly

## User Story
**As a user, I want to combine audio with visual elements so that I can create complete videos**

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] System shall combine voiceover with visual elements
- [ ] Visual elements shall be relevant to script content
- [ ] Video shall be in 1080x1920 format (vertical)
- [ ] Video shall be exported in MP4 format
- [ ] Video quality shall be high and professional

## Definition of Done
- Audio and video are properly synchronized
- Visual elements are relevant and engaging
- Video format is correct for platform
- Video quality meets standards
- Export process completes successfully

## Story Points: 8

## Epic: Video Production

## Dependencies
- FFmpeg or MoviePy integration
- Stock footage API (Pexels)
- Voiceover system
- Video processing infrastructure

## Risks
- Visual elements may not be relevant
- Video quality may be insufficient
- Processing may be too slow
- Audio/video sync issues
