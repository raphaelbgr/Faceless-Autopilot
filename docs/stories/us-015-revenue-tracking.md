# US-015: Revenue Tracking

## User Story
**As a user, I want to track revenue from content so that I can measure ROI**

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] System shall track revenue from content
- [ ] System shall calculate ROI for content
- [ ] User can see revenue by content type
- [ ] System shall track affiliate marketing revenue
- [ ] User can export revenue reports

## Definition of Done
- Revenue is tracked accurately
- ROI calculations are correct
- User can see revenue breakdown
- Reports are exportable
- Revenue data helps optimize content

## Story Points: 5

## Epic: Analytics & Optimization

## Dependencies
- Revenue tracking system
- Affiliate marketing integration
- Reporting system

## Risks
- Revenue tracking may be complex
- ROI calculations may be inaccurate
- User may not understand revenue data
